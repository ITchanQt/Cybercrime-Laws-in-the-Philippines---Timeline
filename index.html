II. Digital Timeline:
Cybercrime Laws in the Philippines

Cybercrime offenses were prosecuted under existing laws (fraud, theft), but there was no comprehensive framework for addressing internet-related crimes.

Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012 (RA 10175): A landmark law that defined cybercrimes, established penalties, and outlined investigation procedures. Driven by the rise of cyber threats.

Challenges and Updates: Debates about freedom of expression and potential for abuse. The law faces ongoing amendments to adapt to evolving cyber threats.
